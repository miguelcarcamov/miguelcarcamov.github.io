<p>My research integrates radio astronomy, computational imaging, and high-performance computing to develop robust end-to-end pipelines for next-generation telescopes. I work on method development, scientific software engineering, and data-driven astrophysical interpretation with an emphasis on reproducibility and scalability.</p>
<ul style="margin-top: 18px; color: #676767; line-height: 1.9;">
  <li><strong>Radio interferometry and image synthesis:</strong> deconvolution, calibration-aware reconstruction, and uncertainty analysis.</li>
  <li><strong>Faraday tomography and compressed sensing:</strong> sparse reconstruction techniques for magneto-ionic structure studies.</li>
  <li><strong>Cosmic magnetism:</strong> observational and methodological tools to characterize extragalactic and circumstellar magnetic environments.</li>
  <li><strong>High-performance scientific computing:</strong> GPU-accelerated and parallel workflows for large astronomical datasets.</li>
  <li><strong>Research software and pipelines:</strong> production-oriented systems for data quality control, reproducible analysis, and publication-ready outputs.</li>
</ul>
